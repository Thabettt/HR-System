# HR System Role Access Matrix

This document outlines the access rights and permissions for each role in the HR System.

## 1. System Admin
**Role Name:** System Admin
**Description:** Full system access, responsible for system configuration and high-level management.
**Permissions:**
- Employee Management: Create, Read, Update, Delete
- Department Management: Create, Read, Update, Delete
- Position Management: Create, Read, Update, Delete
- Role Management: Create, Read, Update, Delete
- Change Requests: Approve
- Reports: View
- System: Manage

## 2. HR Manager
**Role Name:** HR Manager
**Description:** Oversees HR operations including recruitment, payroll, and employee relations.
**Permissions:**
- Employee Management: Create, Read, Update
- Role Management: Create, Read, Update
- Change Requests: Approve
- Payroll: View
- Recruitment: Manage
- Leaves: Manage
- Reports: View

## 3. HR Admin
**Role Name:** HR Admin
**Description:** Handles administrative HR tasks and records management.
**Permissions:**
- Employee Management: Create, Read, Update
- Role Management: Read
- Records: Manage
- Change Requests: Process
- Reports: View

## 4. HR Employee
**Role Name:** HR Employee
**Description:** Supports HR functions, primarily focused on recruitment and candidate management.
**Permissions:**
- Candidate Management: Create, Update
- Employee Management: Read
- Recruitment: Manage
- Change Requests: Process
- Reports: View

## 5. Department Head
**Role Name:** Department Head
**Description:** Manages a specific department and its team members.
**Permissions:**
- Employee Management: Read
- Team Management: Read
- Leave Requests: Approve
- Performance: Manage
- Payroll: View
- Reports: View

## 6. Department Employee
**Role Name:** Department Employee
**Description:** Standard employee role with self-service capabilities.
**Permissions:**
- Own Profile: Read, Update
- Change Requests: Submit
- Own Payroll: View
- Leave Requests: Submit

## 7. Payroll Manager
**Role Name:** Payroll Manager
**Description:** Responsible for overall payroll processing and benefits administration.
**Permissions:**
- Employee Management: Read
- Payroll Management: Read, Update, Process, Export
- Reports: View

## 8. Payroll Specialist
**Role Name:** Payroll Specialist
**Description:** Assists with payroll calculations and administration.
**Permissions:**
- Employee Management: Read
- Payroll Management: Read, Update
- Reports: View

## 9. Recruiter
**Role Name:** Recruiter
**Description:** Focused on talent acquisition and candidate management.
**Permissions:**
- Candidate Management: Create, Read, Update
- Recruitment: Manage
- Interviews: Process
- Reports: View

## 10. Legal & Policy Admin
**Role Name:** Legal & Policy Admin
**Description:** Manages compliance and policy documentation.
**Permissions:**
- Policy Management: Create, Read, Update
- Compliance: Manage
- Reports: View
